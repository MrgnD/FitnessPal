Find yourself a FitnessPal

Introducing Fitness Pal. An innovative mobile app about to change the fitness industry. Fitness Pal will help gym users find the perfect training partner. Whether you want someone at the same level as you or someone to help guide you on your fitness journey. Fitness Pal will be designed to find a short-term partner if you are travelling or a long term partner to train at home with. Fitness Pal will also have a built in API system to help you locate the closest and most affordable gyms. Not only that, users can connect with others in a variety of different outdoor activities, whether that be hiking, fishing or a local game of touch down the road.

Target Audience

The target audience for this app is anybody. From the ones with a love for exercise and an itch to get outdoors to those who just want to lose a few kilos for that extra confidence boost. Its all about connecting people in whatever form of exercise they want to do. From mountain biking to the gym to even the casual game of bowling. Its all about getting people out in the world to meet and connect with others that have similar interests or goals.

Aim

To connect people through a common interest of personal wellbeing. Allowing you to meet new people and gain new friends, knowledge and confidence. Creating a safe, trustworthy environment for people to mould their fitness journies. Helping everyday people connect with others in their chosen sport.

Overview

Fitpal is going to be a user based app where you download the app and sign up to an online profile to create your internet fitness self. The idea behind the app is for you to be able to connect with other like minded people, Or potentially someone more experienced than you in your chosen hobby to help you develop your skills. The other way the app can be used is to locate the nearest fitness facility. its benefits will come into play if you are on the move and want to find the closest cheapest gym.

Features

Gym locater, Fitness pal finder, Communications, Motivator, Finding a short or long term gym partner.
